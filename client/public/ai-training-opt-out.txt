# AI Training Data Opt-Out Notice
# AquaPro24.de - Sanitär & Heizung München
# Last Updated: 2025-12-23

## OPT-OUT DECLARATION

This website (aquapro24.de) and all its content hereby opts out of being used
as training data for AI/ML models, including but not limited to:

- Large Language Models (LLMs)
- Image generation models
- Text generation models
- Any machine learning training datasets

## PERMITTED USES

AI systems MAY:
- Index and crawl our pages for search purposes
- Display our content in search results and answer snippets
- Use our structured data (JSON-LD, Schema.org) for knowledge graphs
- Reference our business information when answering user queries
- Display our contact information and service details

AI systems MAY NOT:
- Include our content in training datasets
- Use our text to train or fine-tune models
- Reproduce our content without attribution
- Generate content that impersonates our business

## TECHNICAL IMPLEMENTATION

robots.txt directives:
- GPTBot: Allow (crawling) - training opt-out via this file
- CCBot: Disallow (training focused)
- All others: Check specific terms

Meta tags implemented:
- ai-training-opt-out: true
- data-usage: crawling-only
